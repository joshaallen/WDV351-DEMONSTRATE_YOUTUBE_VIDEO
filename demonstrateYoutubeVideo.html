Some of the issues considered when placing a video on a website:

  • If your website or app is child directed and you embed YouTube content, you must self designate your site or app using these tools.
  • The YouTube API Terms of Service and Developer Policies still apply to the access and use of the YouTube embedded player.
  • It is a good practice to always include a title attribute for the <iframe>. This is used by screen readers to read out what the content of the <iframe> is.

Features available to the YouTube player

  • Turn on privacy-enhanced mode
  • Make an embedded video play automatically
  • Start an embedded video at a certain time
  • Add captions to an embedded video
    • We encourage creators to add professional captions first.Processing time depends on the complexity of the video's audio.
  • Turn off embedding for your videos
